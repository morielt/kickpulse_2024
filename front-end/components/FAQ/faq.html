FAQs

Where is my order?

If your order has not arrived by the estimated delivery date we’re here to help.

Before contacting us it’s worth checking a few things first. Check the tracking link we sent in your despatch email to view up to date tracking information.

Sign into your account to check we have the correct delivery information for your order and that all your contact details are up to date.

If you still can’t find your parcel contact our Customer Service team quoting your order number.

Contact us via the webform. Select Contact Us from the menu on the left hand side.

Can I cancel my order?

You can cancel your order before it has been dispatched. Please contact our customer service team for assistance.

Can I change the size/colour of the item I've ordered?

Changes to size or colour can be made within 24 hours of placing the order by contacting our support team.

Can I change the delivery address?

Delivery address changes can be requested within 24 hours of placing the order by contacting our support team.

Can I track my order?

Yes, you can track your order using the tracking link provided in your dispatch email.

Part of my order is missing?

If part of your order is missing, please contact our customer service team with your order number for assistance.

What payment methods can I use?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers.

Product Recalls

For information on product recalls, please visit our Product Recalls page or contact our customer service team.

How do I use promotional code?

To use a promotional code, enter it at checkout in the designated field and click apply.

What is cookie?

Cookies are small text files used to store information on your device to enhance your browsing experience.
